Networking and Building Connections through UMSI

How do I connect with other professionals in my industry?

Networking with other professionals can be an intimidating task for students. Here, we have compiled tools, tips, and tricks for connecting with UM alumni, peers, and industry professionals.

Recommended Networking Strategies

  • Build a strong elevator pitch: Practice introducing yourself in a quick 30 seconds to 1 minute. This is very useful for Career Fairs or conferences.
  • Outreach to professionals or UM alumni: Practice outreach through cold emailing professionals, asking for informationl interviews, advice, or connections.
  • Plan an informational interview: Be a connection coffee and talk with them. Focus on building a relationship without being transactional.
  • Prepare for a career fair: University of Michigan hosts at least one career fair each semester. Prepare for it and attend to network in-person!

Don't be afraid to put yourself out there!
